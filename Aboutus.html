我們的理念

建立以「醫院」、「實驗室」、「潔淨廠房」等特殊需求單位為服務對象之專業團隊，經過專業設計及施工技術，達到符合業主特殊機能需求之使用環境，做到真正Environment Control (Envirtrol) System。 To establish professional Interior design team to serve specifically “hospital”, “laboratory”, “clean factory” and so on. With our professional design and construction capabilities, we are confident we can match any special functions for the use of environment requested by our clients to achieve true Environment Control (Envirtrol) system.
